# FinServe — Frequently Asked Questions (Clients)

## General

### 1. What is FinServe?
FinServe is a mid-size European financial services company specialising in lending and credit products for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and retail clients. We offer SME loans, retail credit lines, and invoice financing across the European Union.

### 2. Who can apply for FinServe products?
Our products are available to:
- **Retail clients:** EU residents aged 18 or older with a stable source of income and a valid government-issued ID.
- **SME clients:** Businesses registered in the EU for at least 12 months with annual revenue of at least EUR 50,000.

### 3. In which countries does FinServe operate?
FinServe currently serves clients in Poland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, and France. We plan to expand to additional EU markets by Q4 2025.

## Products

### 5. What types of loans does FinServe offer to SMEs?
We offer SME term loans ranging from EUR 10,000 to EUR 500,000, with repayment periods of 6 to 60 months. The nominal interest rate starts at 5.9% per annum and the APR ranges from 6.8% to 14.2% depending on the risk assessment and loan term.

### 6. What is a retail credit line?
A retail credit line is a revolving credit facility for individual clients. The available limit ranges from EUR 1,000 to EUR 25,000. The nominal interest rate is 8.5% per annum (APR 9.7%–12.4%). You only pay interest on the amount you draw, and the limit renews automatically as you repay.

### 7. How does invoice financing work?
Invoice financing allows SMEs to receive up to 90% of the face value of outstanding invoices within 24 hours of submission. FinServe charges a factoring fee of 1.2%–2.5% of the invoice value plus a monthly interest rate of 0.8% on the advanced amount. Invoices must have a payment term of no more than 90 days.

### 8. Are there any upfront fees?
- **Application fee:** EUR 0 for all products (no application fee).
- **Arrangement fee:** 1.5% of the loan amount for SME loans, EUR 50 flat fee for retail credit lines, 0.5% of the facility limit for invoice financing.
- **Annual maintenance fee:** EUR 0 for the first year; EUR 25 per year for retail credit lines from the second year onward.

## Application Process

### 9. How do I apply for a loan or credit line?
You can apply through our client portal at portal.finserve.eu, by email at applications@finserve.eu, or in person at our Warsaw office. The online application takes approximately 15 minutes to complete.

### 10. What documents do I need to provide?
- **Retail clients:** Government-issued ID (passport or national ID card), proof of income from the last 3 months (payslips or tax declaration), and proof of address (utility bill or bank statement dated within the last 3 months).
- **SME clients:** Company registration certificate, financial statements for the last 2 fiscal years, most recent 6 months of bank statements, government-issued ID of the authorised signatory, and a brief business plan or description of the purpose of the loan.

### 11. How long does the application process take?
- **Retail credit lines:** Approval decision within 2 business days. Funds available within 1 business day after approval.
- **SME loans:** Approval decision within 5 business days. Funds disbursed within 2 business days after signing the loan agreement.
- **Invoice financing:** Facility setup within 3 business days. Individual invoices funded within 24 hours of submission.

### 12. Can I track my application status?
Yes. Log in to portal.finserve.eu and navigate to "My Applications." You will also receive email updates at each stage: application received, under review, decision made, and funds disbursed.

### 13. What happens if my application is rejected?
You will receive a written explanation of the rejection reason within 2 business days of the decision. Common reasons include insufficient credit history, income below the minimum threshold, or incomplete documentation. You may reapply after 90 days or earlier if the reason for rejection has been resolved.

## Repayment

### 14. What repayment options are available?
- **SME loans:** Fixed monthly instalments (annuity) or bullet repayment at maturity for loans under EUR 50,000.
- **Retail credit lines:** Minimum monthly repayment of 5% of the outstanding balance or EUR 25, whichever is greater.
- **Invoice financing:** Repayment occurs automatically when FinServe collects the invoice from the debtor.

### 15. Can I repay my loan early?
Yes. Early repayment is available at any time. The early repayment fee is:
- **SME loans:** 1.0% of the remaining principal if repaid within the first 12 months; 0.5% thereafter; no fee if less than EUR 10,000 remains.
- **Retail credit lines:** No early repayment fee.

### 16. What payment methods are accepted?
Repayments can be made via SEPA direct debit (recommended), bank transfer to the account stated in your loan agreement, or through the client portal using a linked bank account. Card payments are not accepted for loan repayments.

### 17. When is my payment due?
Payments are due on the same calendar day each month as specified in your loan agreement (typically the 15th or the last business day of the month). If the due date falls on a weekend or public holiday, the payment is due on the next business day.

## Fees and Charges

### 18. What happens if I miss a payment?
A grace period of 5 calendar days applies. If payment is not received within the grace period, a late payment fee of EUR 15 is charged, and penalty interest of 4 percentage points above the contractual rate applies to the overdue amount. See our Late Payment Policy for full details.

### 19. Are there any hidden fees?
No. All fees and charges are disclosed in your loan agreement and in our Product Policy document, which is available on our website. The main fees are: arrangement fee, annual maintenance fee (retail credit lines from year 2), early repayment fee (SME loans only), and late payment fee.

### 20. Can I change my repayment schedule?
You may request a schedule modification once per calendar year at no charge. Additional modifications are subject to a restructuring fee of EUR 50. Requests are processed within 5 business days. Contact support@finserve.eu or call +48 22 100 2000.

### 21. What is the maximum interest rate FinServe charges?
The maximum APR across all FinServe products is 14.2% for SME loans and 12.4% for retail credit lines. Invoice financing costs are structured as fees rather than interest and are capped at 2.5% factoring fee plus 0.8% monthly interest on advanced funds.

### 22. How are interest rates determined?
Interest rates are based on the European Central Bank base rate plus a risk margin determined by your credit score, financial history, loan amount, and term length. SME loans also consider the business sector and years of operation.
