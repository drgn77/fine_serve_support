## 1. What Data We Collect

FinServe collects and processes the following categories of personal data:

### 1.1 Retail Clients
| Data Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Identity data | Full name, date of birth, nationality, government ID number |
| Contact data | Email address, phone number, residential address |
| Financial data | Income details, employment information, bank account numbers (IBAN), credit history |
| Transaction data | Loan repayments, credit line drawdowns, payment history |
| Communication data | Emails, phone call records, portal messages, complaint records |
| Technical data | IP address, browser type, portal login timestamps |

All of the above for authorised signatories and beneficial owners, plus:
| Data Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Corporate data | Company name, registration number, registered address, tax identification number |
| Financial statements | Balance sheets, profit and loss statements, bank statements |
| Ownership data | Beneficial ownership structure, shareholder register |

## 3. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Active client data | Duration of the contractual relationship + 5 years |
| Rejected application data | 12 months from the rejection date |
| KYC/AML records | 5 years after the end of the business relationship (as required by AMLD) |
| Complaint records | 5 years from the date of resolution |
| Financial transaction records | 10 years (as required by accounting and tax regulations) |
| Marketing consent records | Until consent is withdrawn + 12 months |

After the retention period expires, data is securely deleted or anonymised within 90 days.

## 4. Technical and Organisational Security Measures

FinServe implements the following measures to protect personal data:

- **Encryption:** All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
- **Access controls:** Role-based access control (RBAC) with the principle of least privilege. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required for all employees and portal users.
- **Network security:** Firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, and regular penetration testing (at least annually by an independent third party).
- **Data centre:** Client data is hosted in EU-based data centres (primary: Frankfurt, Germany; backup: Amsterdam, Netherlands) certified to ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II.
- **Employee training:** All employees complete mandatory data protection training upon hiring and annually thereafter.
- **Incident response:** FinServe maintains a data breach response plan. Breaches affecting personal data are reported to the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours and to affected individuals without undue delay (as required by GDPR Article 33 and 34).
- **Vendor management:** All third-party data processors are vetted for GDPR compliance and bound by data processing agreements (DPAs).

## 5. Client Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

| Right | Description | Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| Right of access (Art. 15) | Request a copy of all personal data FinServe holds about you | 30 calendar days |
| Right to rectification (Art. 16) | Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data | 15 business days |
| Right to erasure (Art. 17) | Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations) | 30 calendar days |
| Right to restriction (Art. 18) | Request that processing of your data be limited | 15 business days |
| Right to data portability (Art. 20) | Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (CSV or JSON) | 30 calendar days |
| Right to object (Art. 21) | Object to processing based on legitimate interest or direct marketing | 15 business days |
| Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7) | Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time | Immediate |

## 6. How to Submit a Data Request

Data subject requests can be submitted through:

| Channel | Details |
|---|---|
| Email | dpo@finserve.eu |
| Portal | portal.finserve.eu → "My Account" → "Data & Privacy" → "Submit Request" |
| Written letter | Data Protection Officer, FinServe S.A., ul. Europejska 12, 00-950 Warsaw, Poland |

### Verification
For security, all data subject requests require identity verification. You will be asked to confirm your identity via the portal (if you have an active account) or by providing a copy of your government-issued ID.

### Response
FinServe will acknowledge your request within **2 business days** and provide a substantive response within **30 calendar days**. If the request is complex or voluminous, the response period may be extended by an additional 60 calendar days, with notification to you by day 30.

## 7. International Data Transfers

FinServe does not transfer personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). All data processing, storage, and backup occurs within the EEA. If any future transfer is required, it will be subject to appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy decision).

---

## 8. Automated Decision-Making

FinServe uses automated credit scoring as part of the application process. Clients have the right to:
- Request human review of any automated decision.
- Receive an explanation of the logic involved in the automated decision.
- Contest the decision and provide additional information.

Requests for human review are processed within **5 business days**.

## 10. Supervisory Authority
