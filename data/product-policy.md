## 1. SME Term Loans

### 1.1 Overview
FinServe SME Term Loans provide fixed-amount financing to small and medium-sized enterprises for business expansion, equipment purchase, working capital, or refinancing of existing obligations.

### 1.2 Loan Parameters
| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| Loan amount | EUR 10,000 – EUR 500,000 |
| Loan term | 6 – 60 months |
| Nominal interest rate | 5.9% – 11.5% per annum (fixed) |
| APR | 6.8% – 14.2% |
| Repayment type | Monthly annuity (standard) or bullet repayment (loans under EUR 50,000 only) |
| Currency | EUR |

### 1.3 Eligibility Requirements
- Business registered in an EU member state for at least 12 months.
- Annual revenue of at least EUR 50,000 (based on the most recent fiscal year).
- No active insolvency proceedings or restructuring.
- Positive credit history (no defaults exceeding 60 days in the past 24 months).
- Authorised signatory must be at least 18 years of age and an EU resident.

### 1.4 Collateral Requirements
- Loans up to EUR 50,000: Unsecured (personal guarantee of the business owner required).
- Loans EUR 50,001 – EUR 200,000: Personal guarantee plus a pledge on business assets (equipment, inventory, or receivables) with a minimum coverage ratio of 120%.
- Loans EUR 200,001 – EUR 500,000: As above, plus a registered charge on commercial real estate or a bank guarantee covering at least 50% of the loan amount.

### 1.5 Fees and Commissions
| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Application fee | EUR 0 |
| Arrangement fee | 1.5% of loan amount (deducted from disbursement) |
| Early repayment fee | 1.0% of remaining principal (first 12 months); 0.5% thereafter; EUR 0 if remaining principal < EUR 10,000 |
| Late payment fee | EUR 15 per occurrence (after 5-day grace period) |
| Penalty interest | Contractual rate + 4 percentage points on overdue amount |
| Restructuring fee | EUR 50 per modification |
| Statement/certificate fee | EUR 10 per document |

## 2. Retail Credit Lines

### 2.1 Overview
FinServe Retail Credit Lines provide revolving credit facilities to individual clients for personal expenses, home improvement, education, or emergency needs. Clients pay interest only on drawn amounts.

### 2.2 Credit Line Parameters
| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| Credit limit | EUR 1,000 – EUR 25,000 |
| Facility term | Open-ended (annual review) |
| Nominal interest rate | 8.5% per annum (fixed) |
| APR | 9.7% – 12.4% (varies with utilisation pattern) |
| Minimum monthly repayment | 5% of outstanding balance or EUR 25, whichever is greater |
| Currency | EUR |

### 2.3 Eligibility Requirements
- EU resident, aged 18 or older.
- Stable source of income: minimum net monthly income of EUR 1,200 (documented via payslips, tax returns, or pension statements).
- No active defaults or court judgments related to unpaid debts.
- Valid government-issued identification and proof of address.

### 2.4 Collateral Requirements
- No collateral required for credit limits up to EUR 25,000. The credit line is unsecured.

### 2.5 Fees and Commissions
| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Application fee | EUR 0 |
| Arrangement fee | EUR 50 (flat, charged once at activation) |
| Annual maintenance fee | EUR 0 (year 1); EUR 25 per year from year 2 |
| Early repayment fee | EUR 0 |
| Late payment fee | EUR 15 per occurrence (after 5-day grace period) |
| Penalty interest | Contractual rate + 4 percentage points on overdue amount |
| Credit limit increase fee | EUR 0 (subject to approval) |

## 3. Invoice Financing

### 3.1 Overview
FinServe Invoice Financing enables SMEs to unlock cash tied up in outstanding invoices. FinServe advances up to 90% of the invoice face value and collects the full amount from the debtor at maturity.

### 3.2 Facility Parameters
| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| Facility limit | EUR 5,000 – EUR 300,000 (aggregate outstanding advances) |
| Individual invoice advance | Up to 90% of face value |
| Maximum invoice payment term | 90 days |
| Factoring fee | 1.2% – 2.5% of invoice face value (per invoice) |
| Monthly interest on advances | 0.8% of the advanced amount |
| Disbursement speed | Within 24 hours of invoice submission and approval |
| Currency | EUR |

### 3.3 Eligibility Requirements
- Business registered in an EU member state for at least 12 months.
- Annual revenue of at least EUR 100,000.
- Invoices must be issued to verified business debtors (no consumer invoices).
- Invoices must not be disputed, encumbered, or subject to set-off.
- Debtors must be located in the EU and have no active insolvency proceedings.

### 3.4 Collateral Requirements
- The assigned invoices serve as collateral. No additional collateral is required for facilities up to EUR 300,000, provided the debtor concentration ratio does not exceed 40% (no single debtor may represent more than 40% of the total facility).

### 3.5 Fees and Commissions
| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Application fee | EUR 0 |
| Facility setup fee | 0.5% of the approved facility limit |
| Factoring fee | 1.2% – 2.5% of invoice face value |
| Monthly interest | 0.8% on the advanced amount |
| Late collection fee | EUR 25 per invoice if debtor payment is more than 15 days overdue |
| Recourse fee | 2.0% of the invoice value if FinServe exercises recourse (debtor default) |
| Statement/audit fee | EUR 10 per document |

## 4. General Provisions

### 4.1 Interest Rate Basis
All fixed interest rates are set at the time of agreement signing and remain unchanged for the term of the facility. Rates are determined based on the European Central Bank refinancing rate plus a risk margin reflecting the client's creditworthiness, loan amount, and term.

### 4.2 Annual Percentage Rate (APR) Disclosure
APR is calculated in accordance with EU Consumer Credit Directive (Directive 2008/48/EC) and includes all mandatory fees and charges. APR is disclosed in the pre-contractual information sheet and in the loan agreement.

### 4.3 Cooling-Off Period
Retail clients have a 14-day cooling-off period from the date of signing the credit agreement, during which they may withdraw without penalty. The client must repay any disbursed funds within 30 days of withdrawal.

### 4.4 Product Changes
FinServe reserves the right to modify product terms for new agreements. Existing agreements are governed by the terms in effect at the time of signing. Clients will be notified of any changes at least 30 days in advance.
